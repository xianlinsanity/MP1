About Us





We are a newly opened restaurant that specializes in Chinese ngohiong and siomai foods. Our restaurant is located in Carcar, Central Visayas, Philippines. We take pride in serving authentic Chinese street food delicacies that are crafted with care and packed with deliciousness.

Our ngohiong rolls are made from the freshest ingredients and are deep-fried to crispy perfection. They are served with a side of puso (hanging rice) and a special sauce that complements the flavors of the ngohiong. Our siomai is also a must-try, made with juicy pork and shrimp filling wrapped in a thin layer of dough.

We believe that good food should be accessible to everyone, which is why we offer our dishes at affordable prices without compromising on quality. Our restaurant has a cozy ambiance that is perfect for casual dining with family and friends.

Come visit us today and experience the authentic taste of Chinese street food delicacies!
